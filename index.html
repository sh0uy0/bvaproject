Our Drive

Forced by war or persecution to flee their homelands, refugees face untold obstacles in gaining the capacity and access to fully operate in a society they are ressestled in.

Our Work

By telling and sharing their stories we aim to to ease the adjustment process by cultivating self efficacy and providing refugees with valuable tools for advocating their interests , building cross-cultural community, informing and engaging the general public.

Our Plan

Through fostering partnerships we look forward to deepen ties with other international organizations as well as new organizational ties in the community.
